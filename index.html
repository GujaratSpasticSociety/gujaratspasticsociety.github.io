Welcome to Gujarat Spastic Society

We are committed to improving the lives of persons with disabilities and supporting their families. Explore our programs, services, and resources to learn more about our mission.

About Us

The Gujarat Spastic Society was established in 1988 with the mission of helping children and adults with disabilities, primarily focusing on individuals with cerebral palsy and related disabilities. Our goal is to promote inclusivity and provide families with the tools and support they need.

Programs & Services

  • Specialized Rehabilitation Services
  • Inclusive Education & Vocational Training
  • Support Groups for Parents & Caregivers
  • Awareness and Advocacy Programs
  • Physiotherapy and Speech Therapy
