About Us

The Gujarat Spastic Society was established in 1988 with the mission of helping children and adults with disabilities, primarily focusing on individuals with cerebral palsy and related disabilities. Our goal is to promote inclusivity and provide families with the tools and support they need.

Over the years, we have grown into a well-respected organization offering a wide range of services, including educational programs, rehabilitation therapy, vocational training, and social integration activities. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure every individual, regardless of their disability, has the opportunity to lead a fulfilling life.

We believe that everyone deserves a chance to thrive, and our work continues to focus on breaking barriers and creating a more inclusive society. Join us in our mission to create a better world for people with disabilities.
